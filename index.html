Our Philosophy

What We Believe

The owners of KidHub firmly believe that building healthy relationships with children requires a foundation of mutual trust and respect. The daily activities will be catered to the interests of the children that attend the program. At KidHub, we believe it is important for children to genuinely enjoy the activities that are being offered and while the children will be encouraged to participate in the different activities they will never be forced. We will always offer free play as an option during our planned activities so that children can join in at their own pace if they wish.

We are committed to helping build each child's self-esteem and self-worth by providing a welcoming and warm environment where children are free to learn new things as they develop their uniqueness and reach their maximum potential. We integrate enhancing leadership skills, connecting to the community and preparing for the future into our program and this is what sets us apart from other programs among many other initiatives that we are involved in at KidHub. Children will be encouraged to get involved in developing ideas for activities to help build their independence, creativity, personal responsibility, teamwork and problem-solving skills.

At KidHub we believe that it is essential for children to be provided with an atmosphere where their physical, social, intellectual, creative and emotional needs are taken seriously and that every effort is made to meet the needs of each child that attends the program. We understand that each child is unique in the way in which their needs are met in these areas and we believe it is our duty as child care professionals to ensure that we discover what each child needs. Our program will promote differentiated learning through a variety of activities (indoor/outdoor, small group, large group, individual, etc.) that will account for the uniqueness of the children who attend.

About the Owners

Who We Are

Rachel & Luke Docksteader were both raised in beautiful Prince Edward Island. Both being driven & business-minded individuals, they decided to combine Rachel’s passion for working with elementary school-aged children and Luke’s years of experience in software engineering to form an exciting school-age program that uses the most up-to-date technology — and KidHub was born in August 2015! They have since grown the business from one after-school program location to providing both before & after school programs in two locations (Sherwood & Cornwall) and offer a variety of other programs including March Break Camps, PD/Storm Day Camps & Summer Camps!

Rachel has a Bachelor of Education (UNB) and a Bachelor of Arts with a double-major in History and Psychology (UPEI). Luke has a Masters of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering (Lakehead University) as well as a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics Engineering Technology (RCC Instititute of Technology).

Rachel & Luke have a beautiful and curious one year old daughter, Mercy, and are expecting their second child in August 2019. They love going on family outings, enjoying the beautiful PEI scenery and spending as much time on the beach in the summer as possible! They have a goofy & lovable Labradoodle, Boe, who loves joining in on the family fun!
