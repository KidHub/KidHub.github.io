About KidHub

KidHub is a premium after-school program designed to cater to the interests of all of the children who attend. Here's what to expect:

Boys and girls from kindergarten to grade six

Safe, fun and relaxed environment for children from kindergarten to grade six

Catered activities

Planned activities catered to the interests of each of the kids

Friendships

Promoting friendship building and fair play!

About the Owner

Rachel Docksteader (Wonnacott)

Rachel has over 10 years of experience working with children in a variety of age groups and settings from being a Child Care Worker to a Supervisor of an After-School Program to being a Substitute Teacher. She has a Bachelor of Education from UNB and a Bachelor of Arts with a double-major in History and Psychology from UPEI.

Rachel has a strong love for working with children and is passionate about planning activities that they genuinely enjoy based on their interests. It is Rachel's firm belief that building healthy relationships with children requires a foundation of mutual trust and respect.
